About Weatherly

Weatherly was born out of a deep fascination with meteorology and a desire to create a tool that empowers individuals to navigate their daily lives with confidence. The journey began with a singular vision: to develop a user-friendly weather application that provides accurate and insightful forecasts.

Throughout the development process, I was driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence. I worked tirelessly to ensure that Weatherly delivers on its promise of providing users with up-to-date weather information in an accessible and intuitive format.

As a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, Weatherly represents the culmination of months of hard work and dedication. I am proud to present this project as a testament to my skills and expertise in web development.
